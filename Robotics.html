FRC Robotics

The 2018 FRC Season was my first time competing in a FRC. FRC is insane, the robots are bigger and better. My favorite moment in FRC was our second match at Kansas City. I film for the team and and was down by the field camera in hand. when a robot hits the side too hard and flips. After it filped it started smoking and the refs called the game. Some how to robot was back up in 5 minuts. We won our first match after that.

Who doesn't like big robots all competing for the same goal. Not to destroy eachother but to score more points. Frc was a great time. I say if you get the chance, do it.
